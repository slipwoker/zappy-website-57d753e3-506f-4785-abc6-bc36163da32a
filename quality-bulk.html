Documentation and Assurance Signals

We maintain transparent practices and provide documentation to support your purchasing decisions. While we work with trusted suppliers, we set clear expectations and provide verifiable information when requested.

COA on Request

Certificates of Analysis are available for most products upon request. These documents detail composition, microbial testing, and batch information from our suppliers.

Traceability Notes

We maintain records linking each product to its source and production batch. This information helps you track ingredients through your supply chain and address quality questions.

Allergen Transparency

Product labels clearly identify major allergens. We can provide detailed allergen statements and cross-contamination information to help you meet labeling requirements.

Storage Guidance

Each product includes recommended storage conditions and shelf life information. Following these guidelines helps maintain quality and ensures consistent performance in your recipes.

All documentation requests can be submitted through our contact page. We typically respond within 1-2 business days with available materials. Some specialty items may require additional coordination with our suppliers to obtain specific test results or certifications.

Typical Spec Snapshot

Exact specifications vary by item and supplier batch. The table below provides representative examples of common ingredient categories, typical pack formats, and handling considerations. For precise specs on any product, please contact us directly—we'll confirm details, allergen profiles, and documentation availability for your specific order.

Ingredient Type Typical Pack Formats Handling Allergen Considerations Documentation
Flour 25kg bags, 50lb sacks, bulk totes Store cool & dry; rotate stock regularly Wheat (gluten); check for cross-contact COA, allergen statement
Cocoa Powder 25kg cartons, 50lb boxes Keep sealed; avoid moisture exposure May contain traces of milk, soy COA, spec sheet, kosher cert
Sugar (Granulated) 50lb bags, bulk bins Store in airtight containers; prevent clumping Generally allergen-free; verify source COA, origin statement
Nuts (Almonds, Hazelnuts) 25lb cases, bulk totes Refrigerate or freeze for extended shelf life Tree nuts; high cross-contact risk COA, allergen cert, origin docs
Oils (Vegetable, Coconut) 35lb pails, 5-gallon containers Store away from light & heat; check expiry Check for soy derivatives; usually nut-free COA, spec sheet, kosher if needed
Extracts (Vanilla, Almond) 1-gallon jugs, 5-liter bottles Store tightly sealed; avoid temperature swings Alcohol-based; check for nut traces in almond COA, ingredient declaration

All specifications subject to supplier availability and batch variation. Contact us for current inventory and detailed documentation.
